About Me

Animator EST.2019 / Programmer EST.2020

I'd like to say that I'm not good at describing myself, but I have the self-conscious to acknowledge that I'm a very peer-driven person. From the very day I began development, it was always for someone else. I first started animating for a dear best friend of mine around 2019, which is what sparked my love for Animating.
Back then, it wasn't about the joyful process of animating, it was the review and uplifting remarks I got from my friends. That eventually led me to fill the role of programmer when they needed one, which is what sparked my love for programming.
I don't enjoy to think that I was left out in favor of a more performative- more composed person, but I can't help but think that way to this day. Though, I do like to boast more about how I've gotten to larger projects with "better pays" and "bigger audiences".
I like to feel important.
